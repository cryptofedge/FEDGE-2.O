# Spotify for Artists

The official platform for managing your Spotify presence, analytics, and promotion.
URL: https://artists.spotify.com

## Core Features

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard
- Real-time streams, listeners, saves, playlist adds
- Audience demographics: age, gender, location
- Source of streams: playlists, profile, search, radio
- Follower growth tracking
- Top songs and albums performance

### 🎵 Playlist Pitching (Editorial)
- Submit unreleased tracks to Spotify's editorial team
- Must submit at least 7 days before release date
- Fill out: mood, genre, instrumentation, language
- One pitch per release — make it count
- Go to: artists.spotify.com → Music → Upcoming

### 📱 Artist Profile Management
- Update profile photo, bio, social links
- Pin a track to the top of your profile
- Artist's Pick — highlight a song, album, or playlist
- Verified checkmark once you claim your profile

### 🎧 Spotify for Artists App
- Mobile dashboard for on-the-go analytics
- Real-time stream notifications
- Available iOS and Android

## Spotify Promotional Tools

### 🎯 Spotify Marquee
- Paid full-screen recommendation card to your listeners
- Appears when fans open Spotify app
- Targets: recent listeners, lapsed listeners, saved users
- Minimum spend: ~$250
- Best used: release week + first 2 weeks after drop
- Access via: artists.spotify.com → Campaigns

### 🔊 Spotify Showcase
- Sponsored recommendation in Spotify Home
- Reaches users who haven't listened to you yet
- Great for fan acquisition
- Minimum spend: ~$100

### 📻 Spotify Discovery Mode
- Opt songs into algorithmic radio and autoplay
- In exchange: reduced royalty rate on those streams
- Trade-off: more reach vs slightly lower per-stream revenue

## Growing on Spotify — Strategy

### Release Checklist
- [ ] Claim your Spotify for Artists profile
- [ ] Submit to editorial playlists 7+ days before release
- [ ] Update Artist's Pick to new song on release day
- [ ] Share Spotify pre-save link in bio
- [ ] Run Marquee campaign during release week
- [ ] Pitch to independent playlist curators (SubmitHub, Groover)

### Key Metrics to Watch
- *Save rate* — saves ÷ streams (aim for 10%+)
- *Playlist adds* — editorial + algorithmic + user playlists
- *Monthly listeners* — overall reach
- *Streams per listener* — engagement and repeat plays
- *Follower conversion* — listeners who follow your profile

### Algorithmic Playlists (Auto-Generated)
- *Release Radar* — new releases to followers every Friday
- *Discover Weekly* — personalized discovery playlist
- *Daily Mixes* — regular listeners hear you more
- Triggered by: saves, follows, playlist adds, repeat plays
