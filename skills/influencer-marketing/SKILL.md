# Influencer Marketing

Strategy and execution for finding, vetting, and managing influencer partnerships.

## Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Best For | Avg Cost |
|------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | Hyper-niche, authentic | Free / gifting |
| Micro | 10K-100K | High engagement, affordable | $50-500 |
| Mid-tier | 100K-500K | Broad reach + engagement | $500-5K |
| Macro | 500K-1M | Mass awareness | $5K-20K |
| Mega/Celebrity | 1M+ | Maximum reach | $20K+ |

*Best ROI:* Micro and mid-tier influencers — higher engagement, more trust, lower cost.

## Finding Influencers

### Free Methods
- Search hashtags on Instagram/TikTok (#reggaeton, #latinmusic, #makeuptutorial)
- Look at who follows similar artists or brands
- Check who's already posting about you organically
- YouTube search for niche content creators

### Paid Tools
- *Upfluence* (upfluence.com) — database + outreach platform
- *AspireIQ* — campaign management
- *Creator.co* — marketplace for micro-influencers
- *TikTok Creator Marketplace* — official TikTok platform (free)
- *Instagram Creator Marketplace* — official Meta platform (free)

## Vetting an Influencer

Before reaching out, check:
- ✅ Engagement rate: likes + comments ÷ followers (aim 3%+ on IG, 5%+ on TikTok)
- ✅ Audience location matches your target market
- ✅ Content quality and aesthetic matches your brand
- ✅ No controversial history
- ✅ Real followers (check for fake engagement: lots of likes, zero comments = red flag)
- ✅ Previous brand deals (are they spammy with ads?)

## Deal Types

### Gifting (Nano/Micro)
- Send free product or music access
- Ask for honest post in return
- No guaranteed post — manage expectations

### Paid Partnership
- Agree on: deliverables, posting date, caption, usage rights, exclusivity
- Always get a contract (use Rocket Lawyer skill)
- Payment: on delivery or split (50% before, 50% after)

### Affiliate/Commission
- Give influencer a unique discount code
- They earn % of every sale they drive
- Low risk for brand, performance-based

## Campaign Strategy

### For Mando El Pelado
- Target: Latin music TikTokers, reggaeton dancers, NYC/Ecuador culture accounts
- Ask: create a duet, dance video, or reaction to "MOMENTOS"
- Goal: get the SOUND used on TikTok → drives Spotify streams
- Look for creators in Ecuador (local support during tour)

### For Sensual Make Up
- Target: beauty micro-influencers, makeup artists, Latina lifestyle creators
- Ask: product review, tutorial, GRWM (Get Ready With Me) using products
- Gift: eyeshadow palette + lip color set
- Goal: UGC content + new customer acquisition

## Tracking Performance
- Unique discount codes per influencer
- Swipe-up links or custom UTM links
- Reach, impressions, saves, shares
- Sales attributed to each creator
- Calculate: Cost ÷ Sales = Cost per acquisition
