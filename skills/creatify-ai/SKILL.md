## Core Features

### 🎬 URL to Video
- Paste any product URL → Creatify generates a complete video ad
- Pulls product images, copy, and branding automatically
- Best for e-commerce (Sensual Make Up Shopify products)

### 🤖 AI Avatars
- Realistic AI presenters for UGC-style ads
- No filming needed — avatar delivers the script
- Multiple voices, languages, styles

### 📦 Product Ads
- Studio-quality product shots and animations
- Clean, professional visuals from product images

### 🎯 AI Shorts
- TikTok-style vertical videos from text + visual style
- Fast content for social media

- Create up to 50 ad variations at once

- Kill underperformers fast
