# Linktree — Bio Link Management

Linktree is the #1 link-in-bio tool. One link → all your content, products, and platforms.
URL: https://linktr.ee

## Why You Need This
Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter only allow ONE link in bio.
Linktree turns that one link into a full directory of everything you want people to find.

### For Music Artists (Mando El Pelado)

## Setup Guide
1. Go to https://linktr.ee or https://beacons.ai
2. Create account with artist/brand name
3. Add links in priority order (most important first)
4. Customize colors to match brand
5. Copy your Linktree URL
6. Paste in Instagram bio, TikTok bio, Twitter bio
7. Update whenever you release new content

## Pro Tip
Pin your most important link at the top and change it regularly:
- Drop week → Stream new single
- Tour → Buy tickets
- Workshop → Book now
