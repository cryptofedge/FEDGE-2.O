# Higgsfield AI — Video & Image Generation

Higgsfield.ai is an AI infrastructure platform for creating high-quality videos, images,
and marketing content. URL: https://higgsfield.ai

- **Seedance 2.0** — Most advanced video model available. High-quality cinematic videos.

- **Kling 2.5 Turbo** — Cinematic VFX, seamless transitions, pro-grade effects

- **Google Veo 3** — Latest Google video generation model
