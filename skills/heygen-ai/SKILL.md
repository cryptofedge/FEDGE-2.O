# HeyGen — AI Video Creation Platform

HeyGen turns scripts, images, presentations, and PDFs into finished videos.
No cameras, no crew, no editing skills required.
URL: https://www.heygen.com

*Stats:* 128M+ videos generated · 102M+ avatars generated · 17M+ videos translated

## Core Features

### 📝 Text to Video
- Paste a script → HeyGen creates a complete video
- AI avatar delivers it in your chosen voice and language
- 1080p or 4K cinematic quality
- Full-length videos, not just short clips
- Voiceovers, visuals, and avatars handled automatically

### 📸 Photo to Video
- Upload any image → becomes a dynamic talking video
- Natural AI lip-sync + expressive facial dynamics
- Add text overlays, background music, transitions
- 175+ languages and dialects

### 🛒 Product Ads / Product Placement
- Upload product image + script
- AI avatar holds your product in the video
- Edit: add music, captions, voiceovers
- Scale TikTok, Instagram, Amazon content without shipping samples

### 🎬 UGC Ads & Reaction Videos
- Create authentic UGC content without cameras
- Product reviews, news updates, story-style videos
- Avatar responds to trending clips
- Perfect for "faceless" channels and personal brands

## AI Avatar Types

### 👤 Photo Avatar
- Upload one static image → brings it to life
- Realistic lip-sync, hand gestures, expressions
- Great for: creating a brand spokesperson from a photo

### 🌍 Public Avatar
- Pre-made diverse AI avatars ready to use
- Professional, engaging — no recording needed
- Great for: training content, explainers, ads

### 🧬 Digital Twin (Most Powerful)
- Film 2-3 minutes of yourself
- HeyGen clones your look, voice, and movement
- Scale your personal brand without recording again
- Great for: Fellito as Éclat Universe face, Mando as artist spokesperson
