# Sensual Make Up — Brand Management

Sensual Make Up is a beauty brand founded by Maria Grafas aka "Corazon".
Website: https://www.sensualmakeup.beauty
Store: https://sensual-makeup-shop-2.myshopify.com

## Brand Identity

- *Mission:* Celebrate confidence, elegance, and self-expression through beauty
- *Vision:* Go-to destination for amplifying confidence through makeup
- *Vibe:* Premium, sensual, empowering — from classic to bold looks
- *Founded by:* Maria Grafas aka Corazon
- *Platform:* Shopify-based e-commerce store

## Product Lines

- *Eyeshadows* — Richly pigmented, wide range of palettes
- *Foundations* — Flawless coverage
- *Lip Colors* — Long-lasting formulas
- *Skincare Collection* — Curated for healthy, radiant complexion
- *Custom/Makeup Workshops* — In-person artistry training sessions

## Marketing Channels
- Instagram (primary social channel)
- Shopify store (e-commerce)
- Website: sensualmakeup.beauty

## Digital Marketing Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. *Product Showcases* — Swatches, tutorials, before/afters
2. *Confidence & Empowerment* — Brand messaging
3. *Behind the Scenes* — Corazon's story, workshop moments
4. *UGC/Customer Features* — Real customers using products
5. *Education* — Makeup tips and tutorials

### Platforms to Focus On
- *Instagram* — Primary. Feed + Reels + Stories
- *TikTok* — Tutorial content, product demos, UGC trends
- *Pinterest* — Beauty looks, mood boards
- *YouTube* — Long-form tutorials, workshop content

### Paid Ads Strategy
- Meta Catalog Ads (dynamic product ads from Shopify catalog)
- TikTok Shopping Ads
- Retarget website visitors via Meta Pixel
- Lookalike audiences from buyer list

### Growth Tactics
- Influencer partnerships with beauty creators
- Makeup workshop promotion (events drive brand awareness)
- Email list building via pop-up on site
- Loyalty/rewards program for repeat customers
- Bundle deals to increase average order value

## E-Commerce (Shopify)
- Store runs on Shopify
- Install Meta Pixel for ad tracking
- Enable Shop Pay for higher conversions
- Set up abandoned cart email flows
- Google Shopping integration for search visibility

## Key Campaigns to Run
- *Product launch* — New eyeshadow palette / lip color drop
- *Holiday* — Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, Christmas sets
- *Workshop promos* — Fill seats for makeup masterclasses
- *Back-to-school beauty* — Late August campaign
