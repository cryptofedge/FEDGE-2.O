# Shopify AI Ad Creation Apps

These apps connect directly to your Shopify store and generate ads automatically from your
product listings — no design experience needed.

---

## 🥇 Promer AI Ads Creative
**Shopify App Store:** https://apps.shopify.com/promer-creative-studio
**Rating:** 4.6/5 ⭐ (31 reviews, 87% five-star)
**Launched:** June 2025
**Developer:** Promer (promer.ai)

### What It Does
- Creates high-converting AI ad creatives for Meta & Facebook directly from your Shopify store
- Pulls product images, titles, and descriptions automatically
- Generates ready-to-launch creatives in minutes

### Best For
- Sensual Make Up — generate product ads for eyeshadows, foundations, lip colors instantly
- Any Shopify store needing Meta/Facebook/Instagram ads fast

### How to Install
1. Go to https://apps.shopify.com/promer-creative-studio
2. Click "Add app" on your Shopify store
3. Authorize access to product data
4. Select products → generate ads → launch to Meta

## Workflow for Sensual Make Up

**Fast launch (under 60 seconds):**
1. Install Promer on Shopify store
2. Select a product (e.g. eyeshadow palette)
3. Promer generates ad creative
4. Push to Meta Ads Manager

**Full campaign (best results):**
1. Use Creatify URL-to-Video with product page URL
2. Generate 10-20 variations with different hooks
3. Launch to Meta + TikTok simultaneously
4. Monitor performance and kill underperformers
