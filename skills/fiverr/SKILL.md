## How to Hire on Fiverr

1. Go to fiverr.com
2. Search for what you need
3. Filter by: Budget / Delivery time / Seller level
4. Check portfolio and reviews before ordering
5. Message seller before placing order for custom projects
6. Place order and track progress

## Seller Levels to Look For
- *Top Rated Seller* — Best quality, highest reliability
- *Level 2* — Proven track record
- *Level 1* — Good starter sellers
- *New Seller* — Lower price but less proven
