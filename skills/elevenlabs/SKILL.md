# ElevenLabs — AI Voice Generation

ElevenLabs is the most realistic AI voice platform. Create studio-quality voiceovers
from text in any language.

## What ElevenLabs Can Do

### 🎙️ Text-to-Speech
- Convert any text to natural-sounding speech
- 3,000+ voices in 32 languages
- Adjust: stability, clarity, style, speed
- Output: MP3, WAV formats

### 🧬 Voice Cloning
- Clone any voice from a 1-minute sample
- Instant clone: quick from short samples
- Professional clone: high quality from longer recordings
- Use cases: consistent brand voice, artist narration, ad campaigns

### 🌍 AI Dubbing
- Translate and dub videos into other languages
- Keep original speaker's voice characteristics
- Great for: translating Mando's content to English/Spanish

### 🎧 Voice Design
- Generate completely new AI voices from description
- "Deep male voice with NYC accent, confident and warm"

- Integrate voice generation into apps and workflows
