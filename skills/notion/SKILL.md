# Notion — Project Management & Team Workspace

Notion is an all-in-one workspace for notes, docs, databases, and project management.
URL: https://www.notion.so

## Why Notion
- Combines: notes + docs + tasks + databases + wikis
- Flexible — build any system you need
- Free for personal use
- Works on all devices
- AI assistant built-in (Notion AI)

---

## Key Features

### 📝 Pages & Docs
- Write anything: meeting notes, SOPs, strategies
- Embed images, videos, files, links
- Comment and collaborate in real time

### 🗄️ Databases
- Table, Board (Kanban), Calendar, Gallery, List, Timeline views
- Filter, sort, and group any way you want
- Link databases together (relational)

### 📅 Project Management
- Task boards with status tracking
- Assign due dates and owners
- Sub-tasks and dependencies
- Timeline/Gantt view for campaign planning

### 🤖 Notion AI
- Summarize long documents
- Draft content from bullet points
- Translate text
- Auto-fill database fields
- Generate action items from meeting notes

## Templates for Éclat Universe

### Content Calendar
- Database with: Platform | Content Type | Status | Post Date | Caption | Assets
- Filter by platform (TikTok, Instagram, etc.)
- Calendar view shows weekly/monthly schedule

### Music Release Tracker
- Database: Song Title | Artist | Release Date | Status | Platforms | Streams
- Track every release from idea to live

### Client CRM (Light)
- Track: Mando El Pelado, Sensual Make Up, other clients
- Notes, meeting dates, deliverables, payment status

### Campaign Hub
- One page per campaign (MOMENTOS launch, Sensual Make Up spring, etc.)
- Goals, timeline, assets, results all in one place

### Weekly Team Standup
- What's done, what's next, blockers
- Recurring template fills itself out
