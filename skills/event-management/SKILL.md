## Ticketing Platforms

### Eventbrite (Recommended)
- URL: https://www.eventbrite.com
- Free for free events
- Paid events: 3.7% + $1.79 per ticket (service fee passed to buyer or absorbed)
- Mobile check-in app
- Email marketing to attendees built-in
- Easy to share and embed anywhere

### Other Options
- *Ticket Tailor* — lower fees, white-label
- *Dice.fm* — popular for music events
- *RA (Resident Advisor)* — electronic/club music events
- *Ticket Fairy* — good for independent artists
- *Cash/Door* — always have backup for walk-ins

## Concert Booking Process

### 1. Pre-Show (4-8 weeks out)
- [ ] Confirm venue, date, time, capacity
- [ ] Sign performance agreement (use Rocket Lawyer skill)
- [ ] Confirm deal: flat guarantee vs door deal vs split
- [ ] Create Eventbrite event page
- [ ] Build promo assets (Canva flyer, social graphics)
- [ ] Start social media promo campaign
- [ ] Reach out to local press and blogs

### 2. Promotion (2-4 weeks out)
- [ ] Run Meta + TikTok ads for ticket sales
- [ ] Reach out to local influencers for shoutouts
- [ ] Email your mailing list (if applicable)
- [ ] Contact local radio stations
- [ ] Add event to all artist profiles (Spotify, Apple Music, Bandsintown)

### 3. Show Week
- [ ] Confirm technical rider with venue (sound, lighting, stage)
- [ ] Arrange transportation and hotel if needed
- [ ] Confirm support acts / opener schedule
- [ ] Set up merch table
- [ ] Brief social media team for live coverage

### 4. Day of Show
- [ ] Sound check (2-3 hours before doors)
- [ ] Set photographer/videographer for content
- [ ] Go live on Instagram/TikTok before/during
- [ ] Post show updates in real time

### 5. Post-Show
- [ ] Share content from the show within 24-48 hours
- [ ] Thank fans, venue, collaborators
- [ ] Collect email addresses (if not already)
- [ ] Invoice venue for payment
- [ ] Review analytics: ticket sales, social reach, revenue

## Performance Deal Types

| Deal | Description | Best For |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Flat Guarantee | Fixed payment regardless of attendance | Established artists |
| Door Deal | % of ticket sales (70-85% to artist) | Building audience |
| Split | Guarantee + % of profit above break-even | Mid-tier shows |
| Pay-to-Play | Artist pays for slot | Avoid when possible |

## Workshop Planning (Sensual Make Up)

- Platform: Eventbrite for ticket sales
- Price range: $50-150 per person for makeup workshop
- Venue: salon, studio, event space, or pop-up
- Max capacity: 10-20 people for intimate experience
- Materials: include in ticket price or sell add-on kit
- Follow-up: email attendees with product discount code
- Content: film workshop → repost as social content
