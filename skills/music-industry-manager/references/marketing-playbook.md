# Marketing Playbook — Social Media & Paid Campaigns

## Content Calendar Template (30 Days)

### Platform Strategy
| Platform | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| TikTok | Short clips, trends, BTS | 1–2x/day |
| Instagram Reels | Polished clips, snippets | 1x/day |
| Instagram Feed | Photos, covers, promo | 3–4x/week |
| Instagram Stories | Polls, Q&A, countdowns | Daily |
| YouTube | Music videos, vlogs, live | 1–2x/week |
| Twitter/X | Updates, engagement, news | 2–3x/day |
| Facebook | Events, longer posts | 3x/week |

## Content Pillars
1. **Music** — snippets, music videos, lyric reveals
2. **Personality** — behind the scenes, day in the life, studio sessions
3. **Fan Engagement** — reposts, Q&A, polls, shoutouts
4. **Credibility** — press features, milestones, Billboard mentions
5. **Lifestyle** — performances, travel, culture

## Paid Ad Templates

### Pre-Save Campaign (Meta Ads)
- Objective: Traffic / Conversions
- Audience: Fans of similar artists (lookalike), interest targeting
- Creative: 15-second snippet reel + pre-save CTA
- Budget: $10–$30/day
- Duration: 2–4 weeks pre-release

### Release Day Push (Meta + TikTok)
- Objective: Reach / Video Views
- Creative: Official music video clip or lyric video
- CTA: "Out Now — Stream Now"
- Budget: $20–$50/day
- Duration: Release week + 2 weeks

### Playlist Pitch Ads (Spotify Marquee / Showcase)
- Use Spotify for Artists Ad Studio
- Target: Current listeners + similar artist fans
- Budget: $250 minimum recommended

## KPIs to Track
- Streams (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube)
- Monthly Listeners growth
- Playlist adds (editorial + user)
- Save rate (saves / streams ratio — aim for 10%+)
- Follower growth rate
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / reach)
- Pre-save conversion rate
- Ad CTR and CPM
