# Outreach Templates

## Booking Inquiry Email
```
Subject: Booking Inquiry — [ARTIST NAME] | [EVENT DATE/TYPE]

Hi [NAME/TEAM],

My name is [YOUR NAME], I'm reaching out on behalf of [ARTIST NAME], 
an independent Latin urban artist based in New York City with roots in Ecuador.

We're interested in discussing a potential booking for [EVENT NAME/TYPE] 
on [DATE] at [VENUE/LOCATION].

[ARTIST NAME] brings high energy live performances with a loyal fanbase 
in the NYC/Latin community. Recent releases include [LATEST SINGLE] 
which has been gaining traction on [PLATFORMS].

You can listen and review his catalog here:
Spotify: [link]
Website: [link]

We'd love to discuss availability, requirements, and compensation. 
Please let us know the best way to move forward.

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
[CONTACT INFO]
```

## Feature/Collaboration Request (to another artist)
```
Wassup [ARTIST],

[ARTIST NAME] here (or reaching out on behalf of). Big fan of your work, 
especially [SPECIFIC SONG/PROJECT] — the [specific element] was crazy.

I've been working on a new record and I think your sound would be perfect on it. 
The track has a [VIBE/GENRE] feel — [1-2 sentences describing it].

Would you be open to linking up on this? I can send the track over for you to 
hear first. No pressure, just think it could be something special.

Let me know 🤝
[ARTIST NAME]
```

Hi [EDITOR/WRITER NAME],

- Latest release: "[SONG TITLE]" feat. [COLLABS]

[NAME]
