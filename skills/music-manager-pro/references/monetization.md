# Artist Monetization Guide — How to Gross More Money

## The Problem Most Independent Artists Have

The music is just the foundation. The real money is in the ECOSYSTEM around the music.

Mando has the content, the story, the look, and the fanbase. He just needs to activate more revenue streams.

## Revenue Stream #1 — Live Performance (Highest ROI)

This is where independent artists make real money NOW.

**Tiers to build toward:**
- Local NYC club shows: $200–$1,000/night
- Corporate/private events (quinceañeras, weddings, corporate Latino events): $1,500–$5,000/show
- Festival appearances (Latin festivals, Ecuador Day NYC, cultural events): $2,500–$10,000
- International (Ecuador/Latin America tours): $3,000–$15,000+ per show

**Action items:**
- List services on website (already done — bookings, features, interviews)
- Create a proper EPK (Electronic Press Kit): bio, photos, video reel, tech rider, past shows
- Target every Ecuadorian community event in NYC, NJ, Connecticut
- Target Latin cultural festivals: Festival Ecuatoriano NYC, Feria de las Flores, etc.
- Partner with Latino event promoters in NYC
- Offer a "bundle rate" — 2 shows booked together = discount
- April 8 THE DROP show = use this as proof of concept for future bookings

## Revenue Stream #2 — Streaming Optimization

Currently underearning on streaming. Fix this.

**Immediate moves:**
- **DistroKid Splits** — make sure publishing splits are set correctly so all co-writers get paid
- **Spotify for Artists** — claim profile, verify, access analytics, pitch to editorial playlists
- **Apple Music for Artists** — same thing
- **Amazon Music for Artists** — register
- **YouTube Content ID** — make sure DistroKid has Content ID enabled. Gets paid every time someone uses his music in a YouTube video
- **TikTok monetization** — once he hits 10K followers, enable creator fund + LIVE gifts

**PRO royalties (free money being left on the table):**
- Register with **ASCAP or BMI** immediately
- Every time his song plays on radio, TV, in a restaurant/bar/store — he gets paid
- Register every song in the catalog, not just new releases
- **SoundExchange** — collects digital performance royalties (Pandora, SiriusXM, internet radio)

- Requirement: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (he's close)
- Once enabled: ad revenue on every video
- At 4.2K subs, this should already be accessible or very close
- Get this activated ASAP

**TikTok Creator Fund / LIVE:**

## Revenue Stream #5 — Features & Collaborations (Paid)

Artists pay for features. He can charge for his verse.

**Rate structure for Mando:**
- Feature verse (independent artist, small following): $300–$800
- Feature verse (mid-level artist, 50K+ followers): $1,000–$3,000
- Promo feature (artist pays for inclusion in his content): $200–$500

**How to get feature requests:**
- List "Features" as a service on the website (already there)
- Post on IG/TikTok: "Feature slots open for [month] — DM for rates"
- Build a Fiverr or SoundBetter profile (marketplace for music services)
