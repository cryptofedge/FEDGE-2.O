# Social Media Strategy — Mando El Pelado

## Current Baseline (as of March 2026)
- **TikTok:** ~2,138 followers | ~9,199 likes
- **YouTube:** ~4,260 subscribers
- **Content observed:** Studio sessions, live performance clips, music promos, Ecuador collabs

---

## TikTok Growth Strategy (Goal: 10K in 90 days)

### The Problem
2K followers with 9K likes = good engagement rate but low reach. 
He's posting but not consistently, and not leveraging trends.

### The Fix: 5-Point TikTok Plan

**1. Post Daily (non-negotiable for 30 days)**
- 1 video/day minimum for first 30 days to feed the algorithm
- Mix: 3 music posts, 2 personality/lifestyle, 2 Ecuador/NYC culture per week
- Best times: 6-9PM EST (NYC audience) and 12PM-2PM (Ecuador audience)

**2. Use the Ecuador/NYC Angle as a Hook**
TikTok loves identity content. His story IS the hook:
- "Growing up Ecuatoriano in NYC vs Guayaquil" content
- "What Ecuadorians say vs what NYers say" style skits
- Behind-the-scenes of balancing both cultures
- Use #paratiecuador🇪🇨 + #Ecuador + #NYC + #LatinNYC on every video

**3. Audio Strategy — Own His Sound**
- Upload every song as an original TikTok sound
- Post the first TikTok using that sound himself (hook clip of best 15 secs)
- Comment on popular videos using his sound with "🔥🙌"
- Ask fans to duet/stitch using his songs

**4. Collab for Cross-Pollination**
- Find 3-5 Ecuadorian TikTokers in NYC with 5K-50K followers
- Offer to make a song together or do a duet/collab post
- Tag all collab artists in posts — their audiences discover him
- Perform "Una Bandida" with Oveja Negra EC via TikTok Live

**5. Content Pillars (rotate these)**
- 🎤 Music — snippets, behind-scenes, recording sessions
- 🇪🇨 Culture — Ecuador pride content, food, slang, throwbacks
- 🏙️ NYC Life — street footage, NYC Latin community moments
- 💬 Story — artist journey, grind, motivational
- 🎬 Trending — hop on trending sounds/formats with Latin twist

## YouTube Growth Strategy (Goal: 10K subscribers)

### The Problem
4.2K subs with official music videos = baseline catalog exists. 
Problem: no consistent uploads, no Shorts strategy, no community posts.

### The Fix: 4-Point YouTube Plan

**1. YouTube Shorts — Post 3x/Week**
Shorts get pushed to non-subscribers. This is the fastest growth lever on YouTube right now.
- Repurpose TikToks directly as Shorts (same vertical video)
- Add YouTube-specific CTA at end: "Subscribe for more 🔔"
- Target 60-second max

**2. Optimize Every Video for Search**
His videos need to be findable:
- Title format: `Mando El Pelado - "[Song Name]" (Official Video) | Reggaeton 2026`
- Description: always include artist bio, all social links, lyrics teaser, relevant hashtags
- Tags: Mando El Pelado, Latin Urban, Reggaeton NYC, Ecuador Music, Urban Latino NYC
- Custom thumbnails: bold text + face close-up = higher click rate

**3. Behind-the-Scenes / Vlog Series**
"Road to THE DROP" — document everything leading up to April 8 show:
- Rehearsals, outfit prep, venue walkthrough, collab sessions
- 1 video/week, 5-10 mins
- This builds parasocial connection = loyal subscribers

**4. Leverage the April 8 Event**
- Film THE DROP show professionally (multi-camera if possible)
- Upload full performance video within 48 hours of the event
- Create highlight reel (3-5 mins) + upload same week
- Go Live on YouTube during the show if possible

## Cross-Platform Playbook

**The Loop:**
TikTok video → repurpose as YouTube Short → post clip on IG Reels → tweet the link

Every piece of content should live on all platforms. One shoot = 4 posts.

**The 3-2-1 Weekly Rhythm:**
- 3 TikToks (music/culture/story)
- 2 IG posts (feed + reel)
- 1 YouTube video or Short

**Growth Triggers to Watch:**
- When a song gets used organically on TikTok → immediately engage with those creators
- When a video hits 10K views → boost it with $20-50 IG/TikTok ad spend
- When follower milestones hit → celebrate publicly to drive more follows
