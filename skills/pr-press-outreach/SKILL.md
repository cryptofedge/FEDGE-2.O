# PR & Press Outreach

Strategic media outreach for artists and brands to earn press coverage and visibility.

---

## Press Release Formula

### Structure
1. *Headline* — Bold, newsy, 1 line
2. *Dateline* — City, Date
3. *Lead paragraph* — Who, what, when, where, why (most important info first)
4. *Body* — Supporting details, quotes, background
5. *Boilerplate* — About the artist/brand (2-3 sentences)
6. *Contact info* — Name, email, phone
7. *###* — End of release symbol

### Press Release Template (Music)
```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

[ARTIST NAME] DROPS NEW SINGLE "[TITLE]" — [ONE LINE HOOK]

[City], [Date] — [Artist Name], the [genre] artist known for [credential],
releases his/her latest single "[Title]" today via [Distributor].
The track [describe in 1-2 sentences].

[Quote from artist about the song/meaning]

"[Song Title]" is available now on all major streaming platforms.
Stream here: [link]

ABOUT [ARTIST NAME]
[2-3 sentence bio with biggest credentials]

MEDIA CONTACT:
[Name] | [Email] | [Phone]

###
```

## Pitching Strategy

### Cold Email Pitch Template
```
Subject: [Artist Name] — New Single "[Title]" + Story Angle

Hi [Journalist Name],

Quick pitch: [Artist Name] just dropped "[Title]" — [1 sentence why it matters].

What makes this story interesting: [unique angle — tour, background, milestone]

[Artist] has [key credential: Billboard mention, X streams, notable show].

Would love to send you the full press release and an exclusive quote.
Happy to arrange a quick interview if interested.

Stream here: [link]
Press kit: [link]

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Building a Media List

Track in a spreadsheet:
| Outlet | Contact Name | Email | Beat | Last Contacted | Response |
|--------|-------------|-------|------|----------------|---------|

*Finding contacts:*
- Check bylines on articles about similar artists
- LinkedIn search: "[publication] music editor"
- Twitter/X: journalists often list contact info in bio
- MuckRack.com — journalist database
- Press release services: PR Newswire, Business Wire (paid)

## Electronic Press Kit (EPK)

Build one for each artist/brand with:
- Short bio (150 words) + Long bio (400 words)
- High-resolution press photos (min 300 DPI)
- Streaming links + sales stats
- Notable press mentions
- Live performance videos
- Contact info
- Social media links

*Tools:* Gamma AI (skill) for beautiful EPK decks
