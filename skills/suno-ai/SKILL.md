# Suno AI — AI Music Generator

Suno is the world's leading AI music platform. Generate complete songs — vocals, lyrics,
instrumentals — from a simple text prompt.
URL: https://suno.com

## What Suno Can Do

### 🎵 Text-to-Music Generation
- Describe a theme, mood, genre, or paste lyrics
- Suno generates a complete song with vocals + instruments
- Professional-grade audio quality

### 🎤 Lyric Customization
- Let AI write original lyrics OR input your own
- Adjust vocal tones, harmonies, and stylistic elements
- Great for creating demos before a full studio session

### ✂️ Song Extensions & Remastering
- Extend existing songs with more verses/bridges
- Upload audio → Suno builds on top of it
- Remaster old tracks with enhanced quality

### 🎛️ Suno Studio (Pro/Premier)
- Stem separation — download vocals, bass, drums separately
- Multitrack editor
- MIDI export
- Advanced production control

### 📱 Mobile App
- iOS and Android available
- Create music on the go
