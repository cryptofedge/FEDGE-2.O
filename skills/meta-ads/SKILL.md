# Meta Ads — Facebook & Instagram Advertising

Meta Ads Manager covers Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Audience Network.
URL: https://www.facebook.com/business/ads

## Why Meta Ads

- 3.2 billion daily active users across Meta platforms
- Most powerful targeting of any ad platform
- Visual-first: ideal for music, beauty, lifestyle brands
- Instagram Reels ads are extremely effective for music promo

## Ad Placements

### Instagram
- Feed posts
- Stories (full screen, 24hr feel)
- Reels (most viral format)
- Explore page
- Profile feed

### Facebook
- News Feed
- Stories
- Reels
- Marketplace
- Right column

## Campaign Objectives
- *Awareness* — Reach, Brand Awareness
- *Consideration* — Traffic, Engagement, Video Views, Lead Gen, Messages
- *Conversion* — Sales, Store Traffic, App Installs

## Targeting (Meta's Strength)
- *Core Audiences* — Age, gender, location, interests, behaviors
- *Custom Audiences* — Upload email/phone lists, website visitors (Pixel), app users
- *Lookalike Audiences* — Find new people similar to existing fans/customers
- *Interest Targeting* — Target fans of specific artists, genres, brands

## Ad Formats
- *Image* — Single photo with copy and CTA
- *Video* — Up to 60 sec (Reels style performs best)
- *Carousel* — Multiple images/videos in one ad (great for product showcases)
- *Collection* — Catalog-based shopping ads (perfect for Sensual Make Up)
- *Stories* — Vertical full-screen

## Budgeting
- Minimum: $1/day per ad set
- Recommended test budget: $30–$50/day
- Let ads run 3–5 days before judging performance
- Key metric: Cost Per Result (CPR)

## Best Practices for Music Artists
- Video/Reels ads with the song playing = best for music
- Target fans of similar artists via interest targeting
- Retarget people who watched 50%+ of previous videos
- Pre-save campaign: Traffic objective → link to pre-save page
- Release day: Reach + Video Views objective
- Follow up with Engagement objective to build social proof

## Best Practices for E-Commerce (Sensual Make Up)
- Use Catalog ads (dynamic product ads)
- Install Meta Pixel on Shopify store
- Retarget website visitors who didn't purchase
- Lookalike from buyer list
- UGC-style video ads outperform polished ads

## Meta Pixel
- Install on website to track conversions
- Essential for retargeting and conversion campaigns
- Easy Shopify integration available
