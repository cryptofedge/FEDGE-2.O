# Stripe — Payment Processing

Stripe is the world's leading payment platform for businesses.
URL: https://stripe.com

## What Stripe Does
- Accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Send professional invoices
- Create payment links (no website needed)
- Set up subscriptions and recurring billing
- Process payouts to bank accounts
- Available in 40+ countries

---

## Key Features

### 💳 Payment Links
- Create a payment link in 2 minutes
- Share via WhatsApp, text, email, Instagram
- No website required
- Great for: service invoices, event tickets, one-off sales

### 📄 Invoicing
- Professional branded invoices
- Automatic payment reminders
- Clients pay online via link
- Track paid/unpaid status
- Export for accounting

### 🔄 Subscriptions
- Recurring billing (monthly, annual)
- Automatic card charging
- Dunning management (handles failed payments)
- Great for: retainer clients, membership programs

### 🛒 Checkout Integration
- Embed payment form on any website
- Works with Shopify, Wix, WordPress
- Optimized for conversion

---

## Fees
- 2.9% + $0.30 per successful card charge (US)
- No monthly fees — only pay when you get paid
- Instant payouts: additional 1% fee
- Standard payout: 2 business days

## Setup Guide
1. Go to https://stripe.com
2. Create account with business info
3. Add bank account for payouts
4. Complete identity verification
5. Create first payment link or invoice
6. Share with client

## Stripe Dashboard
- See all transactions in real time
- Export reports for accounting
- Manage refunds and disputes
- Set up Stripe Atlas to form a US company (if needed)
