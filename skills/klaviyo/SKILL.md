## Core Email Flows (Automated)

### 🛒 Abandoned Cart Flow (Most Important)
- Triggers when someone adds to cart but doesn't buy
- Sequence: Email 1 (1hr) → Email 2 (24hrs) → Email 3 (72hrs)
- Include: product image, price, urgency, discount code on Email 3
- Average recovery rate: 10-15% of abandoned carts

### 👋 Welcome Series
- Triggers when someone joins your email list
- Sequence: Welcome (instant) → Brand story (Day 2) → Best sellers (Day 4) → Offer (Day 7)
- Sets expectations, builds relationship, drives first purchase

### 🏆 Post-Purchase Flow
- Thank you email + order confirmation
- Day 3: How to use / care tips
- Day 14: Review request
- Day 30: Cross-sell related products

### 🔄 Win-Back Flow
- Targets customers who haven't bought in 90-180 days
- Re-engage with: "We miss you" + special offer
- If no response after 3 emails → sunset (remove from list)

### 👀 Browse Abandonment
- Triggers when someone views a product but doesn't add to cart
- Soft reminder email with the viewed product

## SMS Marketing
- Higher open rates than email (98% vs 20%)
- Use for: flash sales, restock alerts, order updates
- Must have consent (opt-in required)
- Keep messages short and direct with clear CTA
