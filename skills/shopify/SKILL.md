# Shopify — E-Commerce Platform

Shopify is the world's #1 commerce platform. Powers Sensual Make Up and millions of brands.
URL: https://www.shopify.com

## Why Shopify

- *15% higher conversions* vs standard checkout
- *250M+* high-intent shoppers via Shop Pay
- 21,000+ apps in the App Store
- Handles everything from startup to enterprise
- Shopify Capital: loans up to $5M for merchants

## Core Features

### 🛍️ Online Store
- Drag-and-drop store builder
- Professional themes (free and paid)
- Mobile-optimized by default
- Custom domain support
- AI-powered store design (Sidekick AI)

### 💳 Payments
- Shopify Payments (built-in, no transaction fees)
- Shop Pay — converts 50% higher than guest checkout
- PayPal, Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Buy Now Pay Later options

### 📦 Products & Inventory
- Unlimited product listings
- Variants (size, color, etc.)
- Digital products support
- Inventory tracking
- Barcode scanning

### 📊 Analytics & Reports
- Sales reports
- Customer reports
- Traffic sources
- Product performance
- Abandoned cart data

### 📧 Marketing Built-In
- Email marketing (Shopify Email)
- Abandoned cart recovery emails
- Discount codes and gift cards
- Loyalty programs via apps
