# Google Ads — Search, YouTube & Display Advertising

Google Ads reaches people actively searching for what you offer.
URL: https://ads.google.com

## Ad Types

### 🔍 Search Ads
- Text ads on Google search results
- Triggered by keywords people type
- Best for: capturing high-intent buyers
- Example: someone searches "buy eyeshadow palette NYC" → Sensual Make Up ad appears

### 🎬 YouTube Ads
- Video ads before/during YouTube videos
- Skippable (after 5 seconds) or non-skippable (15-20 sec)
- Best for: music video promo, brand awareness
- Bumper ads: 6-second non-skippable (great for brand recall)

### 🛒 Google Shopping
- Product listing ads with image, price, store name
- Appears at top of Google search for product queries
- Requires Google Merchant Center + product feed
- Best for: Sensual Make Up product sales

### 📱 Display Ads
- Image/banner ads across millions of websites
- Great for retargeting website visitors
- Lower intent but high reach

### 🎯 Performance Max (PMax)
- Google's AI-driven campaign type
- Runs across all Google channels automatically
- Feed it your assets → Google optimizes placement
- Best for e-commerce at scale

## Campaign Setup Guide

### For Sensual Make Up (Shopping/Search)
1. Create Google Merchant Center account
2. Connect Shopify store (auto-syncs product catalog)
3. Create Google Ads account
4. Link Merchant Center to Google Ads
5. Create Shopping campaign or PMax campaign
6. Set daily budget ($20-50/day to start)
7. Add Google Analytics tag to track conversions

### For Mando El Pelado (YouTube)
1. Create Google Ads account
2. Upload music video to YouTube
3. Create Video campaign → YouTube ads
4. Target: music interests, similar artist fans, Latin music
5. Choose: Skippable in-stream or Video Discovery ads
6. Budget: $10-20/day during release week

## Targeting Options
- Keywords (Search ads)
- YouTube channel/video placement
- Demographics (age, gender, income)
- Interests and habits
- Custom intent (people who searched specific terms)
- Remarketing (people who visited your site/watched your video)
- Similar audiences (lookalike)

---

## Key Metrics
- *CTR* — Click-through rate (aim 3%+ for search)
- *CPC* — Cost per click
- *ROAS* — Return on ad spend (aim 3x+)
- *Conversion rate* — % who buy after clicking
- *Impression share* — % of times your ad could show vs did show

---

## Google Analytics Integration
- Install Google tag on website
- Track: sessions, conversions, revenue
- See which campaigns drive actual sales
- Connect to Google Ads for conversion optimization

## Pricing
- Pay per click (Search/Shopping)
- Pay per view (YouTube — only pay when watched 30+ sec or clicked)
- Minimum budget: $1/day (recommend $20+/day for results)
