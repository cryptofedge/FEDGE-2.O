# TikTok Ads — Advertising Platform

TikTok for Business is the advertising platform for reaching TikTok's 1B+ monthly users.
URL: https://ads.tiktok.com

## Ad Formats

### 🎬 In-Feed Ads
- Appear in the For You Page feed
- Up to 60 seconds, with sound
- CTA buttons (Shop Now, Learn More, Download)
- Most common format for music/brand promo

### 🔥 TopView Ads
- First ad users see when they open TikTok
- Up to 60 seconds, full screen, autoplay
- Highest visibility — best for major launches

### 🎯 Branded Hashtag Challenge
- Sponsor a hashtag trend
- Encourages UGC (user generated content)
- Great for music singles — get people creating content to your song

### ✨ Spark Ads
- Boost existing organic TikTok posts as ads
- Use your own posts or creator content
- Most authentic-feeling format — feels native

### 🛒 Collection Ads / Shopping Ads
- Showcase products directly in TikTok
- Links to Shopify or product catalog
- Great for Sensual Make Up product launches

## Campaign Objectives
- *Reach* — Max eyeballs
- *Video Views* — Push views on a clip
- *Traffic* — Send to website/link
- *App Installs* — Download an app
- *Conversions* — Sales, sign-ups
- *Lead Generation* — Collect emails/info

## Targeting Options
- Age, gender, location
- Interests (music, beauty, fashion, etc.)
- Behavioral (video interactions, creator followers)
- Custom audiences (upload customer list)
- Lookalike audiences
- Hashtag targeting

## Budget & Bidding
- Minimum daily budget: $20/day (campaign level)
- Minimum ad group budget: $20/day
- Bidding: CPM (per 1000 views), CPC (per click), CPV (per view)
- Recommended starting budget: $50–$100/day for testing

## Best Practices for Music Artists
- Use Spark Ads to boost organic content — more authentic
- Target fans of similar artists (behavioral targeting)
- Use the song in the ad — reinforce the track
- 9:16 vertical video, first 3 seconds must hook
- Add lyrics on screen for instant recognition
- Run during release week + 2 weeks after

## TikTok Promote (Simpler Option)
- Boost posts directly from the app without Ads Manager
- URL: https://ads.tiktok.com/business/en/promote-101
- Good for quick boosts, less targeting control
- Min $3/day
