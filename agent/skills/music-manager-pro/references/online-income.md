# Online Income Guide — Making Money Off the Internet

## Website Audit: mandoelpelado.com (Current State)
Built on Wix. Has: Bookings, Features, Interviews, Members section, Blog, Live Stream, File Share, Merch (Printify).

**The problem:** The site exists but isn't set up to CONVERT or EARN passively.
Services say "Price Upon Event" — no price shown = friction = lost sales.
No email list capture. No digital products. No fan monetization.

---

## Website Fixes (Do These First)

### 1. Add Pricing to Services
"Price Upon Event" kills conversion. Post real rates or a starting range.
- Bookings: "Starting at $500 — contact for quote"
- Features: "Starting at $300 — DM for details"
- Interviews: "Free for press / $150 for sponsored"

### 2. Build an Email List (MOST IMPORTANT THING ON THE WEBSITE)
Email list = direct line to fans that no algorithm can kill.
- Add a popup or banner: "Get my new music first — drop your email 🎵"
- Offer incentive: free download, exclusive track, early access
- Tools: **Mailchimp** (free up to 500 contacts), **ConvertKit** (free up to 1K)
- Wix has built-in email marketing — activate it
- Goal: build to 1,000 emails = $1,000+/month potential when monetized

### 3. Add a Digital Store on the Website
Sell directly from mandoelpelado.com:
- Exclusive tracks / unreleased songs: $1–$5 each
- Full EP download: $5–$10
- Signed digital artwork / album cover: $10–$20
- Beat licenses (if he produces): $30–$200
- Wix has a built-in store — just activate it

### 4. Members Section (Already Exists — Activate It)
He already has a Members page. Turn it into a paid fan club:
- Free tier: access to blog + early news
- **Paid tier ($5/month):** exclusive content, behind-scenes videos, early song access
- **VIP tier ($15/month):** monthly 1-on-1 Q&A, exclusive merch discount, name in credits
- Wix Pricing Plans app handles this natively — no extra tools needed

### 5. Live Stream Monetization
He already has a live stream section. Level it up:
- **Wix Events** — sell tickets to virtual concerts ($5–$20 ticket)
- **YouTube Live** — Super Chats (fans pay to have their message highlighted)
- **TikTok LIVE** — virtual gifts from fans
- **Streamlabs** — tip jar during streams (direct PayPal/Venmo donations)
- Monthly "exclusive listening party" for Members = recurring revenue

## Internet Money Streams Beyond the Website

### 6. YouTube AdSense
- He has 4.2K subs — should be monetized already or very close
- Every 1,000 views = $1–$5 in ad revenue
- Posting consistently = compounding income over time
- Activate via YouTube Studio → Monetization

### 7. TikTok Creator Rewards Program
- Replaced the Creator Fund — pays more per view
- Requirement: 10K followers, 100K views in 30 days, 18+
- Get to 10K → apply immediately
- Also: TikTok LIVE Gifts = fans send virtual gifts = real cash withdrawable

### 8. Spotify Songwriter Royalties + Fan Support
- **Spotify for Artists** — once verified, fans can "Support" artists directly
- Not huge money but adds up
- More importantly: Spotify pitches = playlist placement = more streams = more revenue

### 9. Patreon
- Create tiers: $3 / $7 / $15 per month
- Deliver: exclusive content, early drops, behind-scenes, personal shoutouts
- 200 fans at $5 = $1,000/month recurring
- Cross-promote to his Wix Members for dual platform coverage

### 10. BandCamp
- Upload his full catalog to Bandcamp (bandcamp.com)
- Fans pay what they want (or a set price) to download music
- 82% goes to the artist vs 30-35% on most platforms
- "Bandcamp Fridays" — Bandcamp waives their cut on first Friday of each month
- Great for superfans who want to support directly

### 11. SoundBetter & Fiverr — Sell Music Services
- **SoundBetter** (soundbetter.com): marketplace for music talent
  - List as: feature artist, vocalist, songwriter, performer
  - Clients (other artists, producers, brands) hire him for $100–$2,000/session
- **Fiverr**: list vocal features, songwriting, shoutouts
  - Artist shoutout: $25–$100
  - Custom song/verse: $150–$500
  - Guest appearance on track: $200–$800

### 12. NFTs / Digital Collectibles (Optional but growing)
- Mint exclusive tracks or artwork as NFTs on **Sound.xyz** (music-specific NFT platform)
- Sound.xyz lets fans buy "editions" of songs — artist keeps 90%
- Not for everyone, but worth exploring for superfans

### 13. Affiliate Marketing (Easy Passive Income)
- Sign up as affiliate for gear/services he actually uses:
  - **DistroKid affiliate** — earns $$ for every artist he refers
  - **Amazon Music gear affiliate** — link his studio gear in YouTube descriptions
  - **Printify affiliate** — refer other artists to merch platform
  - Add affiliate links to his blog posts and YouTube descriptions

### 14. Blog / SEO (Long Game — Big Payoff)
The blog on mandoelpelado.com is sitting empty. Use it:
- Write 1 article/month about: Ecuadorian music scene, NYC Latin life, making it in music
- SEO target: "Ecuadorian artist NYC," "Latin urban music New York," "Ecuador reggaeton"
- Articles rank on Google → people find his site → discover his music + buy merch
- Monetize blog with Google AdSense once traffic builds

## The Internet Money Mindset

Every fan who finds him online is a potential buyer. The website, TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram are all storefronts.

Right now he's driving traffic to other people's platforms (Spotify, Apple Music) and those platforms keep most of the money.

**The shift:** Drive traffic TO his own ecosystem first.
- His website = his store
- His email list = his direct line to customers  
- His Members section = his recurring revenue
- His YouTube = his TV channel that pays him

When he owns the relationship with the fan, he keeps the money.
